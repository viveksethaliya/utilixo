## Check Your Speed First

**Step 1:** Go to speedtest.net

**Step 2:** Run speed test

**Step 3:** Compare with your plan speed

If speed is significantly lower than your plan, continue with solutions.

## Solution 1: Restart Router

**Step 1:** Unplug router power cable

**Step 2:** Wait 30 seconds

**Step 3:** Plug back in

**Step 4:** Wait 2 minutes for full restart

**Step 5:** Test speed again

## Solution 2: Check Connected Devices

**Step 1:** Open router admin page (usually 192.168.1.1)

**Step 2:** Check number of connected devices

**Step 3:** Remove unknown devices

**Step 4:** Limit bandwidth-heavy activities

## Solution 3: Change WiFi Channel

**Step 1:** Access router settings

**Step 2:** Go to Wireless settings

**Step 3:** Change channel to 1, 6, or 11 (2.4GHz)

**Step 4:** For 5GHz, try channels 36-48

**Step 5:** Save and restart router

## Solution 4: Update Router Firmware

**Step 1:** Check current firmware version in router settings

**Step 2:** Visit manufacturer website

**Step 3:** Download latest firmware

**Step 4:** Upload and install in router admin panel

**Step 5:** Router will restart automatically

## When to Contact ISP

- Speed consistently below 50% of plan
- Frequent disconnections
- Issues persist after all solutions
- Other devices also affected
