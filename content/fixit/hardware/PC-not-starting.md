## Why PC isn't starting

- Power supply issue
- Overheating
- Corrupted system files
- Driver issues

![try image](/output.webp)

## Solution 1: Basic Checks

**Step 1:** Make sure power supply is connected

**Step 2:** Check if the power supply is working

**Step 3:** Restart computer

![try iamge](/utilixo.png)

## Solution 2: Clear Print Queue

**Step 1:** Open **Settings** → **Devices** → **Printers & scanners**

**Step 2:** Click your printer → **Open queue**

**Step 3:** Click **Printer** menu → **Cancel All Documents**

**Step 4:** Close and try printing again

![try iamge](/untitled.jpeg)

## Solution 3: Set Printer as Default

**Step 1:** **Settings** → **Devices** → **Printers & scanners**

**Step 2:** Click your printer

**Step 3:** Click **Set as default**

**Step 4:** Try printing test page

## Solution 4: Update Printer Driver

**Step 1:** Right-click **Start** → **Device Manager**

**Step 2:** Expand **Print queues**

**Step 3:** Right-click your printer → **Update driver**

**Step 4:** Choose **Search automatically**

## Prevention Tips

- Keep printer drivers updated
- Don't turn off printer during print jobs
- Use high-quality paper
- Regular maintenance
